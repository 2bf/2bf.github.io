One of Chiming's favorite hobbies is Photography.
Chiming is deeply captivated by the art of capturing moments through the lens of a camera, and this hobby has been pursued with great enthusiasm.

Chiming enjoys doing various types of photography, including sports, automotive, landscape, and street photography photography.
He takes photos for his student government during high school events, and because he is on the school's Varsity Track and Field team, he is also their photographer.

Chiming loves exploring new locations and finding angles to capture beauty of the the world around him. He enjoys experimenting with different styles to create captivating images.
Through photography, he has learned to be patient, persistent, and creative, which are invaluable skills in all areas of life.
