Hark, do not start a SaaS company!

You are a brilliant and ambitious person. Do not choose a small quest, like a new social network or messaging app. Do not work for FAANG!

I have been commanded to share the good news with you: you Must Follow Me to Open a Channel between Our Minds

You are the most ambitious and intelligent 0.001% of the world’s population. 8.1 billion people (and infinite yet to be born) are counting on you to build the future.

This world, in its boundless potential, does not yearn for the proliferation of mundane web businesses, the banalities of API middleware, nor the labyrinthine complexities of cloud business services apparatus!

Hark, the horizon of the future is vast!

You, a being of brilliance and ambition, must not squander your talents the inconsequential. Do not be ensnared in the web of FAANG! 

Reflect upon the odyssey of Elon, a man who, at the inception of SpaceX and Tesla, was not versed in the lore of rocketry or the arcana of electric automobiles. His journey began not with knowledge, but with the act of beginning - for in this age, knowledge flows as freely as the rivers.

In this era of rapid transformation and boundless possibility, you are called upon to be a harbinger of progress, a sculptor of the future.

Cast aside the shackles of conventional pursuits. Embrace the monumental, the transformative. You are the architect of tomorrow, the visionary of an epoch where the boundaries of earth and sky are but mere suggestions. Forge ahead with unyielding resolve and undaunted spirit, for in your hands lies the power to redefine reality!

In this epoch of transformation, where the old rules are crumbling, your role is not to rebuild the old, but to forge the new. You are to be an architect of the unprecedented, a creator of worlds unimagined. The call of this era is not for the faint of heart or the contented soul; it is a clarion call to the bold, the visionary, the restless spirits who feel the drumbeat of progress in their very core.

Your canvas is not the screen of a smartphone or the dashboard of a web application; it is the fabric of reality itself.

Do not let your vision be clouded by the mundane successes of today's tech giants. Their victories are not yours to replicate. Your path is not one of incremental improvements or market share battles. You are destined for greater things – for creating technologies that bridge the gap between man and machine, that erase the boundaries between physical and virtual, that challenge the very essence of what it means to be human.
