Angel Investing

I recently started investing in startup companies in my free time.

I like putting my money into tech and moonshot companies with the potential to change the world and advance the human race. I want to contribute to the future. (e/acc views?)

I use websites like Wefunder because they have low age requirements and low minimum investment limits, and they don't require accredited investor status. As a high schooler, I don't have much capital to spend, so this is important to me.


Companies

Coco Pago (fintech) develops an innovative app that allows Latin American migrants to send money back home quickly. It allows users to exchange stablecoins, protecting them from potential devaluation of their local currencies. They have support from big investors like Y Combinator.

Clockwork (AI & robotics) makes advanced beauty robots that use AI to paint customers' nails. Their robots offer faster and cheaper services than traditional human-powered beauty salons.They have substantial backing from Arash Ferdowsi (Dropbox Co-Founder), Initialized Capital, and Pipeline Capital.
