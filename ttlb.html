    • (modern) circuses: social media (especially tiktok & short form content), video games, (some) sports, etc.

  • techno-industrialism - software is great, but don't forget about building hard tech and physical innovations.
  • intelligence is our birthright as humans. be grateful for being born with a human brain that out-thinks anything else.
  • as you build, remember you are standing OTSOG (on the shoulders of giants)
