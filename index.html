About me


Hi! I'm Chiming.

In 2023, I started my AI Automation company, Guhoo Technologies.
By implimenting AI into my clients' business operations, I allow them to stay ahead of competition by utilizing the most advanced technologies.

ABE is a helpful tool designed to help users find personalized results for social welfare programs by leveraging AI to match personal information with up-to-date databases of programs.
